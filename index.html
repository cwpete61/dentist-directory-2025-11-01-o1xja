About Dentist Directory

Dentist Directory was founded in 2018 with a simple yet powerful mission: to revolutionize how people find and connect with quality dental care providers. Our founder, a healthcare entrepreneur with over 15 years of experience in the medical industry, recognized a critical gap in the market. Patients struggled to find reliable information about dentists, often relying on outdated referrals or misleading advertisements. We started as a small team of passionate healthcare professionals and technology experts, determined to build a platform that would empower patients to make informed decisions about their oral health. Today, we've grown to serve over 100,000 patients across multiple regions, connecting them with thousands of verified dental professionals.
