Cookies and Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They store information about your browsing activity and preferences, allowing us to recognize you on return visits and enhance your user experience. Cookies may be persistent (remaining on your device until deleted) or session-based (deleted when you close your browser).

Types of Cookies We Use

We use different types of cookies for specific purposes:

  • Essential Cookies:

    Required for basic website functionality, security, and authentication. These cookies cannot be disabled.

  • Performance Cookies:

    Help us understand how visitors use our website by collecting data about page load times, server response times, and error messages.

  • Functionality Cookies:

    Remember your preferences and choices to provide a personalized experience and enhanced functionality.

  • Targeting/Marketing Cookies:

    Used to deliver targeted advertisements and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns. You can opt out of these cookies.

Other Tracking Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use other tracking technologies including:

  • Web Beacons: Tiny graphics that track page visits and user interactions.
  • Pixels: Code embedded in emails and advertisements to track engagement and conversions.
  • Local Storage: Browser-based storage that persists data across sessions.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View what cookies are set and delete them individually.
  • Block all cookies or only third-party cookies.
  • Enable "Do Not Track" signals in your browser settings.

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality and user experience of our Services.
